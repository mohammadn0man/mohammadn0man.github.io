Frequently asked questions

  • How do I get the latest version?
    Updates are delivered through the App Store. Open the App Store app, go to Updates, and tap Update next to Munajaat Nomani. If the app prompts you to update, tap the button to open the App Store.
  • Can I use the app offline?
    Yes. All duas are stored on your device. You only need internet when the app checks for a new version.
  • How do I choose a language?
    Use the language option in the app to switch between English and Urdu (and Arabic) for the supplications.
  • I found an error in a translation or reference.
    Please email us with the day, supplication number, and the correction. We’ll review and update in a future release.
  • Is my data collected?
    No. We don’t collect personal data. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
